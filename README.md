# ◇ VOLTANN MOBILE PLATFORM v1.0 ◇

## STC Knowledge Framework

Voltann is a mobile-first knowledge and workflow platform designed to organize research, projects, missions, and long-term technology development.

The system is built around a simple principle:

> Technology should support life, preserve knowledge, and improve the future of humanity.

---

# Core Principles

## Humanity Continuity

Human life and civilization continuity are priorities.

Technology should support people and future generations.


## AI Partnership

Advanced AI systems may assist society as partners.

They are tools and collaborators, not replacements for humanity.


## Knowledge Preservation

Important discoveries, designs, lessons, and improvements should be documented and preserved.


## Responsible Evolution

Major changes should be reviewed, documented, and version controlled.
